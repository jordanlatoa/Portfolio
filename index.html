About

Welcome to the place where my work lives.

From a young age I've always had a desire to use and explore what I can create with a computer. Throughout college my passion resulted in finishing at the top of my class for Information Technology, where I majored in Web Design for Certificate II and Certificate III. Since then I’ve gained familiarity with C# .NET and Python.

I have always had an excited interest in filming, design and editing with the content varying from local and international travel experiences, to my early skateboard videos, to business and marketing projects. My exposure to new editing techniques has really made me appreciate and admire the work of editing. You can find some of my recent video edits here, along with Procreate and Photoshop work here.

To get in touch please email me at latoawork@gmail.com
